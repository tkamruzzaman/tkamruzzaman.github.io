About Me

Working as a Game Developer for the last 6 years with the main focus on creating fun free to play games. I made several successful casual and hyper-casual games with different concepts and art styles. I worked in different game genres, making prototypes from concepts, implementing different game mechanics, improving code performance, collaborating with peers, and setting the difficulty curve for game balancing through playtesting and game data analysis.

I love to play different kinds of games. I am dedicated to my work and enjoy learning through new challenges.

Portfolio

My game Dropple (4M+ downloads), Stairway (2M+ downloads), Twisted Blockz, and Tilt Ball got featured in Google Play Store as well as Carpenter in App Store.
