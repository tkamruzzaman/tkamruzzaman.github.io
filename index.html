    Smoking Bad

    3D low-poly side-scrolling puzzle platformer game.

    Our team, Karigor, won the Bangladesh National Finals of the Microsoft Imagine Cup 2015 in the Game Category with our game Smoking Bad to create awareness about the harmful effects of smoking. The game centers around Aurora, a young girl whose father faces cancer due to smoking. In her dreamland odyssey, Aurora strives to rescue her father by navigating through five distinct levels: Hospital, Village Landscape, Quarry, Forest, and Underground Cave. Each completed level grants a symbolic 'heart,' and after accumulating five hearts, Aurora can save her father within her dream. The game unfolds with a cinematic cutscene, setting the stage for this heartfelt journey of approximately 2.30 hours of gameplay.

    Contribution to the project:
    • Conceptualized two levels, comprising all puzzles within them.
    • Designed 3D props and puzzle pieces using Blender.
    • Level design with puzzles and props in Unity.
    • Programmed puzzle mechanics and interactions using C#.
    • Aided in capturing and processing 'Mocap' data through Microsoft Kinect.
    • Enhanced the game with particle effects.
    • Edited sounds using Audacity.
    • Produced promotional video content using Blender.
